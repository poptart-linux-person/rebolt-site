Frequently asked

How does multiplayer work?
One player hosts a room and shares a short room code; up to several friends can join with that code, no accounts needed. You can also play solo against AI.

The Monster caught me — is the run over?
Not for the whole team. Getting caught only takes you out; you drop into spectator mode and can keep watching until the run ends for everyone.

How do I play as the Monster instead of a survivor?
In the Lobby screen, switch your role to "The Monster" before the match starts.

My multiplayer connection isn't working.
Rebolt connects players directly to each other (peer-to-peer). If a join keeps failing, try switching off a restrictive Wi-Fi network (some corporate/school networks block the required connections) or use cellular data instead.

Privacy

Rebolt does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Room codes and gameplay state are only relayed directly between players for the duration of a match.
